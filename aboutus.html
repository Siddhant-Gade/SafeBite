Safe Bite: Internationally Declared Harmful & Banned Substance Identifier With Nutrition Analysis

Meet the team behind the innovation

Overview

A team of six ambitious first-year students from Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune, embarked on a journey to develop an impactful project that could benefit society at large. Initially, they explored various innovative ideas, ranging from technological advancements to automation solutions. However, they soon realized the significance of creating something that directly addresses public health and safety. With a shared vision of contributing to consumer well-being, they conceptualized Safe Bite: Internationally Declared Harmful & Banned Substance Identifier With Nutrition Analysis. This groundbreaking initiative aims to empower individuals by identifying banned or harmful ingredients in food products while also providing detailed nutritional analysis. Recognizing the increasing complexity of global food regulations and health concerns, the team sought to bridge the gap between consumer awareness and food safety standards. Their dedication to leveraging technology for the greater good led to the development of Safe Bite, a platform designed to enhance transparency, promote healthier choices, and ensure compliance with international food safety norms.
